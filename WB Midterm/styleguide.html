Here's some context about my interactive site, Esoteren... The word "Esoteren" comes from the adjective "esoteric", which is defined as: intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with a specialized knowledge or interest. Esoteren is the name of the resistence band that the user joins upon starting the game.

User is woken up by a member of the resistance, told he/she was unconscious above ground, where it was too dangerous. Resistance explains the story of how the man-eating bots came to be, how mankind was demolished, and how there is a way to fight back . . . through knowledge. The goal of the game is to attain the knowledge by making the right decisions and to use it to find and kill the main bot monster.
